### 2. Market Relevance Scoring
AI evaluates the resume against current market trends:

- **Overall Score (0-100)**: Comprehensive market fit assessment
- **Trending Skills**: Skills currently in high demand
- **Outdated Skills**: Technologies losing market relevance
- **Missing Skills**: In-demand skills not present in resume
- **Detailed Analysis**: Narrative explanation of market position

### 3. Job Matching System
Match analyzed resumes against specific job descriptions:

#### Match Score (0-100%)
Overall compatibility percentage based on:
- Skills alignment
- Experience level
- Education requirements
- Industry fit

#### Skills Gap Analysis
- **Matched Skills**: Skills that align with job requirements
- **Missing Skills**: Required skills not in resume (with importance rating)
- **Additional Skills**: Bonus skills that add value

#### Experience Fit
- Experience level match score
- Relevant industry experience
- Role progression analysis
- Leadership experience evaluation

#### Strengths & Weaknesses
- Key advantages for this role
- Areas of concern or gaps
- Competitive differentiators
- Development opportunities

#### Recommendations
- Hiring decision guidance
- Interview focus areas
- Onboarding considerations
- Training suggestions

#### Fit Analysis
- **Technical Fit**: Ability to handle technical requirements
- **Cultural Fit**: Alignment with company values and work style
- **Growth Potential**: Career development trajectory

#### Detailed Reasoning
Comprehensive narrative explaining why the candidate is (or isn't) a good fit for the role, including:
- Specific examples from resume
- Comparison to job requirements
- Risk assessment
- Long-term potential
